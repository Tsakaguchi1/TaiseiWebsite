My Favorite Top 4 Games

Nier: Automata

An action role-playing game developed by PlatinumGames. A game with amazing narrative that takes place in post-apocalyptic world where the protagnist who is an android must eradicate robots who settled in Earth to bring back humanity to its original home. The story takes quite a twist and turns with amazing gameplay that is developed by PlatinumGames.

Dead by Daylight

An asymmetrical multiplayer horror game developed by Behaviour Interactive. The charm of this game derives from the fact that the game offers several collaborations with horror film characters that are playable.

Final Fantasy XII

Final Fantasy XII - The most well-known JRPG series but this installment is considered as a niche and underrated, yet remains to be one of my childhood favorites.

Resident Evil 2 Remake

An action horror shooter game developed by one and only Capcom. This game is the faithful remake of the original Resident Evil 2 released back in 1998, which made it's comeback in 2020. The gameplay offers elements of horror that were not present in the original game through use of highly revamped graphics and features.
